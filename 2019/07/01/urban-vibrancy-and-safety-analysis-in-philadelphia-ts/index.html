Introduction

After EDA on Philadelphia crime dataset, I will do time series analysis to predict the quantity of crime in 2019 under the data of 2006~2018 in this notebook.

Data Analysis and Visualization

Glance on Data

From the figure, there is a trend on crimes by Day or Month, but not year.

Non-violent crime

From the figure, we can find that there is no significant trend on non-violent crime.

violent crime

From the figure, we can find that there is better trend on violent crime than that on non-violent crime. Thus, I will do time series on violent crime data.

Time Series Analysis

Model Selection

Firstly, I decompose the trend of violent crime to original, seasonal and residual.

Next, I will do Dickey-Fuller test and plot ACF and PACF.

From the result of ACF and PACF, we could have two possible model, $ARIMA(2,0)$ and $ARIMA(2,1)$. And I try these two model.

From the result, we can find that the first model $ARIMA(2,0)$ is better.

Prediction

Therefore, I will use $ARIMA(2,0)$ to predict the number of crime on Dec 2019.

We can find that the result is not very good, and there is a significant trend, so I will decompose it.

The result is much better.

Summary

In this case, I use Time Series method to predict the number of crimes, and I think the procedure is more complex than the ML model. Perhaps, the ML model is a better choice. This need to be verified in the future.
